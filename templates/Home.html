Ei shundor bachcha ti ke chenen?
Ei mishti chele ti holo amader ajker din er chotolok bondhu Drohan.

Drohan er jonmo hoy ek borolok barite. Kintu nijer biri khawar nesha or pocket money puron korte na parae, shey kola pata roll kore bhetore hajmola-r guro bhore biri phoke.

Drohon er chotobela thekei priyo bishoy holo roshayon karon o rosh nie khelte khub bhalobashe, ebong shobar gaye rosh chhetay.

Or jiboner porom uddesho holo "gachh lagao pran bachao" --- nijer gachh er obhab er dukkho bholanor jonno ei poth beche nieche shey. Shudhu gach-e noy, poshu der proti o tar opar bhalobasha. Tar chagol er proti eto prem je seh asto ekkhana pnathhar mangsher dokan khule phelechhe jekhane nijer pachhar mangsho ke mutton bole bikri kore shey.

Rosho-shastre tar parodorshita nidarun. Tar banano CUM-molom jekono dag, jala, chulkuni ke nimesh er moddhei binash korte pare. Loker bishshash Drohan shoyong Kaamdeb er ee obotar. Shey 2109 khana jouno-mudra-r porom sroshta.

Shey haga ke ekti nikrishto kormo bole mone kore, ebong tai roj hagte hagte hanuman chalisa pore ja die tar bhogobaner proti porom bhokti phute othe. E chharao ei mohan byakti gondhorbo bidda, orthath, gaan er khub onuragi. Tar din shuru ebong sesh hoy Tony Kakkar er "knata laga uii ma" diye. Prottek din sondhe belae, jokhon shara Kolkata-r akash 'Star Jalsha'-r serial er songlape gom-gom kore, tokhon Drohon shei mormo-sporshi obhinoy dekhe nirobe kaade.

"Choto jontro, boro shopno" -----
Matro aathero bochor boyoshe tin lokkho bachcha jonmo diye ebong shob naamjada jibon-boiggyanik der kotha bhul proman kore, ei banir jibonto udahoron hoye uthechhe Drohan.

Drohan-potni Rifa - khubi boro moner ek mohila. Tar udarotar phole Drohan dar chhoto jontro take mene nite ektuo oshubidhhe hoyni ebong shey biyer por tar shob shopno shotti korar kotha dieche.

Drohon da jokhon depressed hoe jae, tokhon Rifa di shobsomoe tar pashe thake ar dorkar porle dada ke "Chupa-Chups lollipop" diye anal daye, ja theke dada porom shukh labh kore ebong oti shohojei depression katiye othe.

Rifa didi nirontor Bio-shadhonay mogno, tar Kaamdeb tullo porom-potir jontrer meramoti korbar jonne. Biology chorcha kore shey Bio-debi upadhi peyechhe Institute of Bio-shadoks theke.

Potir jaate koshto na hoy tar jonne ghumabar age Drohan da ke "Bhai tomar boro hobe" gaanti geye shonay amader Bio-debi. Rifa di nijer churanto parodorshita obolombon kore ek nidarun molom srishti korechhe Drohan dar jonno - shotti ekei bole turu lobh, potir koshto laghob korte asto ek-khana oshudhi baniye fello Bio-debi.

Molomtir naam --- "Dicko Turmeric WSO Cream". Ei molom ti holud guro ar buro Abesh er bomi diye toiri. Ei molom laganor pore "air-contact" "minimize" korbar jonyo, gachher chhal diye toiri "jacket" poriye rakhte hoy Drohan dar jontre. Tarpor shei jacket upor diye narkel-dnori bnedhe fan er shathe tan-tan kore bnedhe Drohan ke chhute berate hoy ghorer modhye - noile e molom er karjokarita kome jaay bolei goponshutre khobor peyechhi amra. Tai jonnoi Drohan dar erokom karjokolap.

Ei shokoler bodole amader mohan Drohan da emon jinish Bio-debi ke shekhay ja Bio-debi chhotobela theke korte parena - "Deshlai jalano", ja die amra shokolei shohojei bujhte pari shei molomtir nidarun agni nirbapon khomota.

Tobe amader bishshash oi narkel dnorir motoi shokto bhabe Rifa di ar Drohan dar haat chirodin shokto hoye dhore thakbe eke oporke.
